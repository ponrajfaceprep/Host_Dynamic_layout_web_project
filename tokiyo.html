TOKIYO

Tokyo, the capital of Japan, is a dynamic metropolis blending traditional culture with cutting-edge modernity. It is home to historic landmarks such as the Meiji Shrine, the Imperial Palace, and the ancient Senso-ji Temple, alongside towering skyscrapers and futuristic architecture in districts like Shinjuku and Shibuya.

Renowned for its culinary excellence, Tokyo boasts more Michelin-starred restaurants than any other city, as well as vibrant street food markets. The city also offers serene gardens like Shinjuku Gyoen and Ueno Park, contrasting with its bustling shopping districts and vibrant nightlife.
